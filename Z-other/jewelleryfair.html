Delhi Jewellery & Gem Fair 2024 Shines with Record Participation, Showcasing 150,000+ Exquisite Design

New Delhi, 29th September 2024: Informa Markets in India, the nation’s foremost B2B event organizer, triumphantly concluded the 12th edition of the Delhi Jewellery & Gem Fair (DJGF) at Pragati Maidan, New Delhi. This year’s edition, the largest in the event’s history, dazzled attendees with over 650 exhibitors, presenting an extraordinary selection of traditional and contemporary jewellery designs from 700 pioneering brands.

Boasting more than 150,000 unique pieces, the event offered an unparalleled display of craftsmanship, featuring a diverse array of materials such as gold, silver, diamonds, pearls, and gemstones, alongside cutting-edge innovations in jewellery design and production technologies. A special highlight was the DJGF Silver Show, located in Hall No. 2, showcasing a captivating array of silver jewellery. Additionally, one of the largest collections of gold, Polki, and diamond jewellery was spotlighted, with esteemed brands from the natural loose diamond sector taking centre stage.

As the ultimate platform for distinguished jewellers, import-export merchants, and key industry stakeholders, DJGF drew over 25,000 professionals, setting the stage for the upcoming festive season, including Navratri, Dussehra, Diwali, and the all-important wedding season—a time of heightened demand in the jewellery sector across India.

The grand inauguration of the event saw a stellar lineup of dignitaries, including Mr. Pramod Derewala, Chairman of NGJIC; Mr. Naveen Kumar, Chairman of TBJA; Mr. Ashok Sheth, Regional Chairman (North India) for GJEPC; Ms. Nirupa Bhatt, a leading Gems and Jewellery Industry Expert; Mr. Yogesh Mudras, Managing Director of Informa Markets in India; Ms. Pallavi Mehra, Senior Group Director, Informa Markets in India; and Mr. Pankaj Shende, Group Director, Informa Markets in India, among other notable industry leaders.

Expressing his pride in the event, Mr. Pramod Derewala, Chairman of NGJIC, stated, “It was a privilege to be part of this prestigious gathering. The role of marketing and design in driving our industry’s growth cannot be overstated, and I applaud the government’s initiatives, which have given a tremendous boost to our sector. I also commend the organizers for spotlighting silver and promoting handcrafted jewellery, which encapsulates the rich cultural heritage of India.”

Recognizing the impact of the Retail Jewellers Guild Awards, Ms. Nirupa Bhatt, Gems and Jewellery Industry Expert, remarked, “Beyond the impressive business activity on the show floor, I was inspired by the additional features, particularly the Retail Jewellers Guild Awards, which provided an outstanding platform to highlight talent and bring more attention to our industry. The Shakti recognition, honouring women’s contributions, was especially significant, as women’s involvement is key to our sector’s evolution. Their perspectives in design and merchandising are crucial to sustaining our industry’s success.”

Mr. Yogesh Mudras, Managing Director of Informa Markets in India, echoed these sentiments during his opening address, stating, “The Delhi Jewellery & Gem Fair captures the dynamism and innovation that define India’s jewellery industry. With gem and jewellery imports soaring to US$ 1.55 billion in June 2024, this year’s edition proudly showcased designs that reflect India’s rich heritage while emphasizing the industry’s growing global influence. This platform opened new horizons for wholesalers, retailers, manufacturers, and investors alike.”

The theme, “Celebrating Craftsmanship and Innovation,” underscored the seamless blend of traditional artistry with modern techniques. Attendees participated in engaging panel discussions on topics such as ‘Women in Power, Women Empower,’ ‘The Future of Responsible Sourcing: Challenges and Opportunities,’ and ‘The Changing Landscape of the Jewellery Industry.’ Presentations spanned areas such as customer experience in retail jewellery, top retail and colour trends shaping the market, and new opportunities in the gems and jewellery export sector.

Renowned thought leaders and influencers such as Ms. Hetal Vakil, jewellery designer, educator and entrepreneur; Navin Sadarangani, founder of NYUZ; social media influencer Prerna Makharia; Mr. Rahul Desai, CEO & MD of IIG; Ms. Swati Sahay, Head of Marketing & Strategy for South Asia at FashionSnoops.com; and Mr. Amit Mulani, Managing Director of Atlantico International, added their insights to the discussions.

Continuing its tradition of honouring industry excellence, the DJGF hosted the Retail Jewellers Guild Awards, recognizing outstanding jewellery retailers across various categories. A glamorous Fashion Show Night further showcased pioneering brands and their latest collections. Among the event's highlights was the SHAKTI ceremony, which celebrated the contributions of women in the jewellery industry, recognizing their vital role in shaping the future of this vibrant sector.

Aligned with Informa Markets in India’s 5th ‘Festivity of Business’ campaign, the DJGF integrated seamlessly with the season’s vibrant series of signature events running from September to December, fostering connections, meaningful dialogue, and collaborative relationships that drive the industry’s collective growth.

The DJGF not only celebrated the artistry and innovation at the heart of India’s jewellery industry but also embodied the spirit of collaboration that will continue to fuel the sector’s success and global impact.
